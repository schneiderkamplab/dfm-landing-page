Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2025

Danish Foundation Models ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This policy outlines how we collect, use, and store your data.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data:

  • Contact form: Name, email address, message content, and subject
  • Newsletter: Email address and consent confirmation
  • Website analytics: Non-personal usage data (e.g., page views, browser type)

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your personal data solely for:

  • Responding to inquiries submitted via the contact form
  • Sending newsletters if you've explicitly consented
  • Improving our website and services

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) — for newsletters and contact forms
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — to improve our site and understand user engagement

4. Storage and Retention

  • Contact messages and newsletter signups are stored securely in internal logs
  • Your data is not shared with third parties
  • We retain contact data only as long as needed to fulfill its purpose or until you request deletion

5. Your Rights (Under GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Danish Data Protection Authority (Datatilsynet)

Contact us at: petersk@sdu.dk

6. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use third-party cookies or tracking technologies unless explicitly stated.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.
